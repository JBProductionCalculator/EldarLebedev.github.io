We provide Architects, Designers and Developers with 3d-rendering services. With photorealistic renders our Clients can present the full potential of their projects in the best possible light.

URBANIDAD Studio is the ARCHITECTURAL VISUALIZATION Department of EDDA, an independent brand design agency operating on the market since 1997. URBANIDAD Studio is a team of creative professionals with a customer-centric approach and the fundamental principle - quality in everything. Not only do we provide our clients with high-end visualizations, but we also have exceptional customer service and project management that guarantees the smooth and on-time execution of each project.
We work to show the beauty, balance, and soul of each space you design. Merging your design and our talent we can create amazing visualizations that will fulfill their purpose. In URBANIDAD Studio we pay special attention to lighting and textures, which makes it easy to mistake a visualization for a photo.
Whenever create a design, let us make your project a reality caught by a virtual camera.
